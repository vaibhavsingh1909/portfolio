About Me

I'm a Product Designer based in Pune, India, with a background spanning SaaS, fintech, and Web3 — places where design has to earn its complexity.

My work lives at the intersection of UX strategy and interaction craft. I care deeply about accessibility, scalable design systems, and the kind of UX writing that makes interfaces feel human.

Lately I've been exploring AI-powered design tooling — using Figma Make, Claude Code, and Lovable to accelerate workflows without sacrificing quality.

Trained as a mechanical engineer, I bring a systems mindset to every problem I touch.
